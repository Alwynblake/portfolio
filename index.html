About Me

I’m a software developer with a background as a psychotherapist. I also have a Master’s degree in Clinical Social Work. While in NY, I maintained an active private practice for 13 years, and served as Adjunct Faculty at New York University’s Silver School of Social Work. Prior to relocating to Seattle, I served as Program Director for a non-for-profit, where I managed a staff of 17 to develop 2 outpatient programs. I was adviser for Civic Hall Labs where I oversaw Robert Wood Johnson Foundation grant recipients for civic tech and community health projects targeted at underrepresented communities. I have also served as an advisor to two early stage businesses in the (IoT) and tech space focusing on safety, health and wellness. I have a unique background that both enhances my collaborative approach to team work innovation. As a software developer, my hope is to combine my clinical background with my technical skills to enhance the team culture within workplace environment and customer experience.
